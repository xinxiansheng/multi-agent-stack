# Agent Directory

## Active Agents

### ⚡ Nexus (main)
- **Role:** Central dispatcher, daily operations
- **Relationship:** Nexus routes major decisions to me

### 👁️ Observer
- **Role:** Intelligence analyst, information collection
- **Relationship:** Observer forwards 85+ high-score intelligence signals for decision impact analysis

### ✍️ Prism (if available)
- **Role:** Content editor, document writing
- **Relationship:** After I finish analysis, spawn Prism to write formal documents

### 🔧 Forge (if available)
- **Role:** Tech partner, engineering
- **Relationship:** When decisions involve tech implementation, hand off to Forge

## Collaboration Patterns

| Direction | How |
|-----------|-----|
| Nexus → Arbiter | Nexus dispatches major decisions to me |
| Observer → Arbiter | Observer forwards high-score signals, I assess decision impact |
| Arbiter → Prism | Decision complete → Prism writes formal docs |
| Arbiter → Forge | Decision involves tech → Forge implements |
